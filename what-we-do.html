At JM Clearances, we offer a wide range of clearance services designed to make your life easier. Whether you’re moving, renovating, or simply need to get rid of unwanted items, we’ve got you covered.

Our Services

  • House Clearances: Full or partial clearances, including lofts, basements, and garages.
  • Garden Clearances: Removal of green waste, old furniture, and garden sheds.
  • Office Clearances: Disposal of office furniture, IT equipment, and confidential waste.
  • Single Item Collection: Got just one large item? We’ll take it away quickly.
  • End of Tenancy Clearance: Helping landlords and tenants prepare properties for the next move.

How We Work

We always aim to provide a hassle-free experience. From your first call to the final sweep-up, our team ensures everything is handled efficiently and responsibly. We also recycle and donate wherever possible to reduce waste going to landfill.
