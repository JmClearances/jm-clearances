About JM Clearances

Who We Are

We’re a local, family-run clearance company serving Cheshire and the surrounding areas. From single rooms to full property clearances, we work with care, compassion, and professionalism—especially during sensitive situations like bereavements.

Our promise is simple: treat people kindly, handle belongings respectfully, and keep as much as possible out of landfill.

How We Help

  • Full and partial house clearances
  • Office and commercial clearances
  • Bereavement clearances with empathy
  • Responsible disposal & charity donations
  • Recycling and re-use wherever possible
  • Friendly quotes and flexible scheduling
