Who We Are

JM Clearances is a trusted clearance company serving Chester, Warrington, Wrexham, Northwich, Ellesmere Port, and surrounding areas. We pride ourselves on delivering professional and affordable clearance services while treating every property and customer with respect.

Our Values

We believe in working with care, compassion, and integrity. From bereavement clearances handled with sensitivity to office clearances carried out efficiently, our team is committed to providing a service that makes the process as stress-free as possible.

Eco-Friendly Approach

We are passionate about protecting the environment. Many of the items we collect are donated to charity or recycled, helping to reduce waste and give back to the local community.
