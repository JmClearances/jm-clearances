About JM Clearances

We are a family-run clearance company serving Cheshire and the surrounding areas. We provide house clearances, office clearances, and bereavement clearances with care and compassion.

Our team works responsibly, donating reusable items to charity and ensuring that all waste is disposed of in an eco-friendly way. We aim to recycle as much as possible to protect our environment.

Whether you need a full property cleared, an office prepared for new tenants, or respectful help with a bereavement clearance, JM Clearances will support you every step of the way.
