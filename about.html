About Us

At JM Clearances, we provide reliable and affordable clearance services. From full house clearances to garden and office removals, no job is too big or small.

We are a licensed and insured waste carrier, ensuring everything is disposed of responsibly. Whenever possible, we recycle and donate items to local charities.

We pride ourselves on delivering a professional, friendly, and respectful service — especially for bereavement and sensitive clearances.
