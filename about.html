About JM Clearances

We are a family-run clearance company serving Cheshire and the surrounding areas. Our goal is to provide stress-free, eco-friendly, and compassionate clearance services for homes, offices, and sensitive situations such as bereavements.

Why Choose Us

  • 1. Who We Are: A small, dedicated team focused on providing a personal touch to every clearance.
  • 2. Our Mission: To make clearances easy, affordable, and eco-conscious. Compassion first, landfill last.
  • 3. Responsible Disposal: Recycling and donations always come before landfill. We work with charities to give items a second life.
  • 4. Peace of Mind: We leave properties clear, tidy, and ready for their next chapter.

“We started JM Clearances to help families, landlords, and businesses in our community by offering a reliable, compassionate, and eco-friendly service. We’re proud to serve Cheshire with a difference.”

— JM Clearances Team
