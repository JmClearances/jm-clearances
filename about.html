About Us

Who We Are

JM Clearances is a local, family-run business dedicated to helping people clear their homes, offices, and gardens quickly and responsibly. We pride ourselves on being reliable, affordable, and environmentally conscious.

Our mission is simple: to make the clearance process stress-free and ensure items are disposed of or recycled responsibly wherever possible.
