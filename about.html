About Us

At JM Clearances, we specialise in professional clearance services across Chester, Warrington, Northwich and surrounding areas.

We understand that every clearance is different. Whether it’s a full house clearance, office clearance, or sensitive bereavement clearance, our team handles each job with care, respect, and professionalism.

What sets us apart is our commitment to being eco-friendly. Wherever possible, we donate reusable items to local charities, ensuring that belongings are given a second life instead of going to waste.

Our experienced team takes pride in offering a reliable, discreet, and efficient service — making the process as stress-free as possible for our clients.

If you’re looking for a trusted local clearance company that combines professionalism with compassion, JM Clearances is here to help.
