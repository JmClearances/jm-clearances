About JM Clearances

Who We Are

Local, Compassionate & Eco-Friendly

We’re a friendly, family-run clearance company serving Cheshire and the surrounding areas. Whether it’s a single room or a full property, we work with care, compassion, and professionalism—especially during sensitive situations like bereavements.

Our promise is simple: treat people kindly, handle belongings respectfully, and keep as much as possible out of landfill.

Our Values

Compassion, Respect & Sustainability

We don’t just clear houses and offices—we provide peace of mind. Every item is sorted carefully: reusable items are donated to charity, recyclables are processed responsibly, and waste is kept to a minimum.
