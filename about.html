About Us

JM Clearances is a trusted clearance company serving homes and businesses across the UK. With years of experience, we’ve built a reputation for reliability, professionalism, and great value for money.

We are committed to being eco-friendly. Whenever possible, we recycle items and materials to reduce waste. Items that are in good condition are donated to local charities, helping communities and reducing landfill.

Our mission is simple: to take the stress out of clearing unwanted items. From single rooms to entire properties, we handle every job with care and efficiency. We’re here to provide a hassle-free service, leaving spaces clear, clean, and ready to use.
